## Company Overview

### Quick Facts
- **Name:** Dreamwell AI
- **Founded:** 2022
- **Headquarters:** Toronto, Canada
- **Team Size:** 2-10 employees (8 on LinkedIn)
- **Industry:** AI-powered influencer marketing SaaS
- **Funding:** $2.3M USD pre-seed (August 2024)
- **Lead Investor:** Tim Draper (Draper Associates)
- **Co-Investors:** Panache Ventures, BDC Capital Seed Venture Fund

### The Problem They Solve
**60% of influencer campaigns fail to break even**

Brands are:
- Overcharged by influencers with inflated engagement metrics
- Unable to accurately value influencer partnerships
- Wasting ad-spend on campaigns that don't convert
- Manually managing complex influencer relationships

### The Solution
AI-powered platform that automates influencer marketing from discovery through performance tracking, focusing on **ROI over vanity metrics**.

**Key Stat:** Automates up to **90% of influencer campaign setup work**

## Competitive Landscape

### Main Competitors

#### GRIN
- **Pricing:** $2,500-$10,000/mo (annual commitment)
- **Strengths:**
  - eCommerce native
  - Product seeding to commission-based sales
  - All communication under one roof
  - Payment and contract management
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Limited discovery (relies on first-party authentication)
  - Small creator pool
  - Need separate tool for recruitment

#### Upfluence
- **Pricing:** ~$2,000/mo (annual commitment)
- **Strengths:**
  - 12M+ creator database
  - Shopify and Amazon integrations
  - Identifies customers who are influencers
  - Real-time sales and affiliate tracking
  - Jace AI integration
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Users report limited influencer pool
  - Difficulty finding new options

#### AspireIQ (Aspire)
- **Pricing:** ~$2,300/mo
- **Strengths:**
  - Creator relationship management focus
  - Branded application pages
  - Whitelisting and UGC tracking
  - Content approval CRM
  - Strong Shopify integration for product seeding
  - Hands-on support
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Higher cost
  - More focused on long-term partnerships than quick ROI

### Dreamwell's Competitive Advantages

1. **AI-First Automation** - 90% of campaign setup automated
2. **Pricing Transparency** - Proprietary formulas vs. inflated metrics
3. **ROI-Focused** - Revenue over vanity metrics
4. **More Affordable** - Especially with current pricing
5. **Founder-Market Fit** - Ex-influencer who understands both sides
6. **Speed to Value** - Quick campaign setup and execution
